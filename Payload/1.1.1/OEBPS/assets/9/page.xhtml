Oro came back down the hill.

He could tell something was wrong.

He walked in circles around Lorenzo,

humming loudly.

He nudged Lorenzo

with the side of his head.

He grunted and snorted.

Finally he calmed down

and stood still.

Lorenzo was scared.

The pain was getting worse.

He knew it would be dark soon,

and it would not be safe to walk.

He would be stuck on the hillside

all night in the cold.
