The next morning,

Lorenzo took some string.

He tied it to both ends of the panpipe.

Then he hobbled outside

and called for Oro

Proudly, he hung the panpipe

around Oro’s neck.

“Gracias, Oro. Thank you,” he said,

giving the llama a big hug.

“You are worth far more than gold!”
