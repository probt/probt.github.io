Lorenzo often took walks with Oro.

Together they would walk up

into the high mountain meadows.

Lorenzo loved the bright wildflowers.

Oro loved the fresh grass.

Today as they walked,

Lorenzo played his panpipe.

The clear notes sailed on the wind.

The baublestied to Oro’s ears bounced

to the music.
